Welcome to Mama's Pie Shop

Our History

For many years, Martha Jean has been baking the most delicious pies at her home. In 2012, we openeded a store in Memphis, Tennessee in a grourgeous venue near the Grand Market. People from all over the South fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, mouth-watering apple pies...The store became a success in no time.
In 2019, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Martha Jean's Pie Shop moved into a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your own home and from anywhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order Martha's delicious pies whenever you feel like it!

Martha Jean is famous for using only the best ingredients. This way, we can make sure that our pies are absolutely the best.
