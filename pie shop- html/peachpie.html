Description

How we make this pie

  1. The crust: We prepare either pie crust recipe through step 5.
  2. Prep the peaches: We peel the peaches and then cut into 1-inch chunks. We need about 8 cups of peach chunks, which is around 1.4kg. Cover and refrigerate until ready to make the filling.
  3. In a large bowl, stir the peach chunks, granulated sugar, flour, lemon juice, ginger, and cinnamon together until thoroughly combined. Set filling in the refrigerator.
  4. Adjust the oven rack to the lower third position and preheat to 425°F (218°C).
  5. Roll out the chilled pie dough: out the chilled pie dough: On a floured work surface, roll out one of the discs of chilled dough (keep the other one in the refrigerator). Set aside at room temperature or in the refrigerator as we assemble the top pie crust.
  6. Arrange the lattice: Remove the other disc of chilled pie dough from the refrigerator. Roll the dough into a circle that is 12 inches diameter.Carefully thread the strips over and under one another, pulling back strips as necessary to weave.
  7. Bake the pie on the center rack at 425°F for 20 minutes; then, keeping the pie in the oven, reduce the oven temperature down to 375° (190°C).
  8. Remove pie from the oven, place on a cooling rack, and cool for at least 4 hours before slicing and serving.
